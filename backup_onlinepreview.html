ABOUT ORDERING THE BOOKS

Can I preorder any of the books featured in the preview?

Sure! Just fill out the order form and send it to Scholastic:
By mail: Scholastic Library Publishing, PO Box 3765, Jefferson City, MO 65102
By fax: (866) 783-4361
By email: slpservice@scholastic.com
For customer service, call toll-free (800) 621-1115
In Canada, visit www.scholastic.ca

You can also send the order form to any library vendor of your choice.
Books will be sent when available.

Are the prices listed for each title discounted for libraries?

No, the prices in the catalog and order form are the full “list” price for each book. When you order, you will receive whatever discount your vendor offers to libraries. If ordering direct from Scholastic Library Publishing (see above), you will receive 40% discount on “trade” titles.

Will the previewed books be available through Scholastic’s book fair catalog, so we can use our Scholastic dollars?

The titles presented in our preview are new titles from our “trade” publishing imprints. Some of them may be available through Scholastic’s bookfair catalogs at a later time.
